MSOE AI Chatbot

I collaborated on a project to develop an AI chatbot prototype tailored for Milwaukee School of Engineering (MSOE). The aim was to address the specific need of facilitating student access to the university’s health and support services. This project spanned over a semester in a group with one other person. My role as the team lead involved:
  • Coordinating meetings and meeting plans
  • Managing project objectives and deadlines
  • Help design and prototype the AI chatbot
Our tasks involved conceptualizing the user experience, designing the chatbot interface, and prototyping its functionality. To achieve this, we made the choice to focus on the specific use case of helping students connect to MSOE’s health and support services. These services include health care from a nurse practitioner, mental health counseling, and academic support services. Utilizing skills in user research, persona development, UX/UI design principles, conversation design, and prototyping tools such as Figma and Voiceflow chatbot builder. The goal was to create an intuitive and user-friendly chatbot that seamlessly integrated with MSOE's existing systems.
List of tasks I was responsible for completing:
  • Conducted user research to understand the expectations of MSOE students regarding access to health services.
  • Conceptualized a persona representing the target user and designed an iconic representation for the chatbot.
  • Utilized Figma to outline conversation flows, ensuring consistency with MSOE's branding guidelines.
  • Employed the Wordflow chatbot builder to prototype the chatbot's functionality, including features like appointment scheduling, FAQs, and emergency contacts.
  • Iteratively tested the prototype with potential users, gathering feedback to refine the user experience and improve functionality.
  • Prepared a comprehensive presentation showcasing the capabilities and potential impact of the chatbot, tailored specifically for a representative from MSOE's marketing department.
The AI chatbot prototype resulted in several positive outcomes:
  • The chatbot prototype effectively addressed the specific need of facilitating student access to health and support services at MSOE.
  • The collaboration between team members helped the development of a well-rounded solution that met both user needs and organizational requirements.
  • The presentation and demonstration of the chatbot prototype to the MSOE marketing department received positive feedback, highlighting its potential impact on improving student experiences and engagement.
  • Through this project, I gained valuable experience in user research, conversation design, prototyping, and collaboration.
Overall, the MSOE AI chatbot project provided valuable insights into the practical application of user experience principles and methodologies in addressing real-world problems within educational institutions.
