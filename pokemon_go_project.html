Pokémon GO Design System

As part of our Design Systems class capstone project, our entire class worked as a team to undertake the challenge of enhancing the user experience of Pokémon GO, a popular augmented reality mobile game. This project required us to leverage our skills in communication design, information architecture, visual design, and interface design to develop a cohesive design system for the game. The project duration spanned an entire semester, and I collaborated with a dynamic design team consisting of four members.

Our design team’s primary tasks involved contributing to the aesthetic elements of our design system for Pokémon GO and developing two pivotal features aimed at improving user connectivity and gameplay dynamics. To accomplish this, we utilized skills in communication design, information architecture, visual design, and interface design, along with tools such as Adobe XD and Figma for prototyping. The goal was to enhance the overall user experience of the game while maintaining its core identity and gameplay mechanics.

List of tasks I was responsible for completing:
  • Collaborated with team members to analyze the existing user experience of Pokémon GO and identify areas for improvement.
  • Utilized communication design principles to establish a cohesive visual language and design patterns for the game's interface elements.
  • Created user personas and journey maps to provide a shared understanding for the target users expectations and understanding of the game
  • Contributed to the development of an information architecture framework to organize game content and features in a logical and intuitive manner.
  • Designed and prototyped a streamlined friend-finding mechanism using Adobe XD, aimed at improving user connectivity and social interactions within the game.
  • Iteratively tested the prototypes with potential users to gather feedback and refine the designs for optimal usability and effectiveness.
  • Compiled the results of our efforts into a comprehensive presentation and demonstration, showcasing the project's process, outcomes, and the rationale behind design decisions.

The Pokémon GO Design System project yielded several positive outcomes:

  • The creation of a cohesive design system provided a unified visual identity and consistent user experience across different aspects of the game.
  • The development of the friend-finding mechanism and battle interface features contributed to improving user connectivity and gameplay dynamics, enhancing the overall experience for players.
  • The presentation and demonstration of our prototypes effectively communicated the project's process, outcomes, and the value of design decisions to stakeholders and peers.
  • Through this project, I gained valuable experience in design systems development, prototyping, user testing, and presentation skills.

In summary, the Pokémon GO Design System project demonstrated the practical application of a design system utilizing principles and methodologies in enhancing the user experience of a popular mobile game, while also providing valuable learning experiences and skill development opportunities.
