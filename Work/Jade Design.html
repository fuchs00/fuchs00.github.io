Jade Design
Group project for a visual design class in spring of 2022 where we created a fictional design firm. Our group was tasked to make a logo and style guide and I created an envelope and letterhead design for the business.
Logo & Style Guide
Envelope & Letterhead
Our group wanted this business to be elegant and have an "earthy" feel that inspired change and growth. We made the logo and style guide to portray these feelings. With the envelope and letterhead designs, I wanted to keep them simple and use colors from the style guide in a way that made it better for print.
