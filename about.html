  • My Brand

    My personal brand aesthetic is a visual narrative that encapsulates my experiences, relationships, and growth. This choice is more than just a preference for over-sprayed or dripping paint. It is a homage to a significant chapter of my life.
    For several years, I have been a part of the paint crew in my apartment building, a role that has not only developed some of my skills but also enhanced my life in unexpected ways. Starting as an entry level member and eventually becoming the lead for the crew, this experience has been a canvas where I painted not just walls, but also my character and worldview.
    The dripping paint in my design symbolizes the dedication that has seeped into my work ethic as I navigated through the challenges of my role. This journey has gifted me with some of my closest friendships, mentors, and memories. Each drop of paint in my design is a tribute to these relationships and serves as a visual echo of my belief that like a well-painted canvas, a well-designed user experience can leave a lasting impact.
