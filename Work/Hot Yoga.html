Hot Yoga Milwaukee
Hot Yoga Milwaukee is a yoga studio that had a poster that wanted to be redesigned. The poster outlined proper etiquette in the studio but it didn't grab the attention that they wanted.
Poster Sketch & Edits
Redesigned Poster
The goal for my redesign was to have the poster grab people's attention better and emphasize the most important information. The studio wanted to keep the poser on 8.5" x 11" paper so I removed redundant information, rotated the poster to a landscape orientation, and highlighted important information.
